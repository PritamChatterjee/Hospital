Billings, Insurance, & Financial Assistance

Massachusetts General Hospital is dedicated to ensuring patients understand the hospital's billing and insurance policies and financial assistance options.


Find information about Massachusetts General Hospital's billing and insurance policies for patient hospital stays, procedures or doctor's appointments.


  • Billings
    • Patients who receive care at Cambridge Hospital or the Cambridge Hospital Physicians Organization will be billed through Partners HealthCare. Learn more about the billing process and financial assistance, read frequently asked questions about billing

  • Insurance
    • Learn what you need to know before scheduling an appointment or hospital visit. Partners HealthCare provides patients with a list of health insurance plans accepted at CBH. Cambridge Hospital accepts most health insurance plans.
  • Financial Assistance
    • Partners HealthCare can assist patients who have limited resources to pay their medical bills for treatment at Cambridge Hospital. There are several forms of financial assistance available.

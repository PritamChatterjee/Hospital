Tissue & Organs Donation ↡
Helping to connect patients with lifesaving organ transplants and tissue donations.

The Cambridge Hospital works with the New England Organ Bank to facilitate organ, tissue and eye donations.

By working together, CBH and New England Organ Bank ensure that all medically suitable patients can help others through organ and tissue donation.

The New England Organ Bank is the oldest independent organ procurement organization in the country and is the federally designated organ procurement organization for all or part of the six New England states.

Women's Health



Helping Women of All Ages Stay Healthy



Women are the foundation of our families and communities. To help women stay healthy, we offer expert care at 5 area locations - everything from routine screenings to advanced treatments. Here's why many women choose CBH for their care:

The Convenience
You can choose a provider at Cambridge Hospital or a care center
Evening appointments at several sites
Coordinated care and quick connection to specialists or tests you need

The Choices

You can choose a great OB/GYN doctor or Family Medicine doctor
Or, see a Midwife at our hospitals, birth center, or community care centers

The Services

Routine Outpatient Care
Ob/Gyn services and screenings for women of all ages
Other services like family planning, nutrition and office procedures

Expert maternity services & newborn care, including traditional and centering models of prenatal care with two wonderful options for families, our Cambridge Hospital maternity unit and our Cambridge Birth Center right across the street
Excellent gynecological surgical services
Expert menopause care
State-of-the-art testing services, like digital mammography and bone density testing
Access to specialists in areas like Neurology, Surgery, Orthopaedics and our award-winning mental health programs
Our nationally-accredited Breast Center and Breast Imaging Center offering a comfortable, secure setting

The Staff

You will find doctors, physicians assistants, nurse practitioners, nurse midwives and doulas who are experts in their field
Our friendly nurses and support staff have dedicated their careers to the health of women

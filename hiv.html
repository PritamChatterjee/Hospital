HIV Prevention and Services



Cambridge Hospital is committed to reducing the number of HIV infections in our communities, as well as providing medical care and social services to those who are HIV positive. We accomplish these goals through outreach efforts that includes distributing language-appropriate materials, HIV counseling and testing, and by providing primary care. In all services, strict confidentiality is maintained.

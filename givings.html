Cambridge Hospital has a charity and we depend on the public's support. Give today and help us improve the future of health care.
For many years now, Cambridge Hospital’s accomplishments in lifesaving patient care, leading-edge medical research and superb caregiver education have been nurtured by the legacy of its committed donors. Your tax-deductible gift to Cambridge Haspital will help fund programs that give patients access to the latest advanced technologies, clinical trials and promising treatments.

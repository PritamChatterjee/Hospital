Careers


As a community healthcare system,you can work at the Cambridge, Somerville or Whidden Hospital campuses. We consider applicants for all positions without regards to race, color, creed, religion, national origin, age, disability, marital status, or any other legally protected status.


Working at Cambridge Hospital

  • Leadership.
    Help CHA fulfill its academic mission by teaching the next generation of medical professionals.

  • Transformation.
    Help develop innovative care models like the Patient-Centered Medical Home.

  • Research.
    Investigate broad community health issues.

  • Innovation.
    Be on the forefront of new technologies like our system-wide electronic health record.

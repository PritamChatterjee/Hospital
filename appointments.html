Appointments & Referrals

Request an appointment or second opinion, refer a patient, find a doctor or view test results with Cambridge Hospital's secure online services.

STEP 1: Register as a Patient

If you have not previously seen a doctor at the Cambridgr Hospital or within the Partners HealthCare Network, please call the CBH Registration & Referral Center to register.

☎ 999-211-6588


Step 2: Contact a Doctor

Request an appointment by calling our appointment agency and ask for an available specialist doctor with your preferrence about what you may want to have an appointment for.

☎ 999-764-9980


Contact Your Health Care Provider or Access Center Directly

To request an appointment with the Cambridge Hospital doctor, contact that doctor directly.

OR

Use Patient Gateway

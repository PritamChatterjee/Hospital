Awards & Recognitions


Each year, Cambridge Hospital is recognized for achieving the highest standards in patient care, education and research, ranking the hospital among the most prestigious medical institutions.


Gold Performance Achievement Award-Stroke
CBH has received the American Stroke Association's Get With the Guidelines Gold Performance Achievement Award. This award honors the hospital for implementing best practices for stroke care and ensuring stroke patients receive the highest quality care according to nationally accepted standards and recommendations.


DiversityInc's Top 10 Hospital Systems for Diversity
CBH was included in DiversityInc's 2016 Top 10 Hospital Systems, recognized for our exceptional CEO commitment; targeted grant, mentoring and development programs; and programs to promote cultural competency and address health disparities.


LGBT Healthcare Equality Index, 2016
Cambridge Hospital is recognized by the Human Rights Campaign Foundation for its commitment to equitable, inclusive care for LGBT patients and their families.


Accredited Vein Center by the Intersocietal Accreditation Commission (IAC)
The Cambridge Hospital Associations have achieved accreditation from the Intersocietal Accreditation Commission (IAC) for vein treatment and management.


Foster G. McGaw Prize for Excellence in Community Service, 2015y
The Cambridge Hospital received the 2015 Foster G. McGaw Prize for Excellence in Community Service, one of the most esteemed community service honors in health care.
